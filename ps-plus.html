Frequently Asked Questions

How does the shared primary account work?

+

You'll receive login credentials for a PS5 account that's set as your console's primary account. This gives you full access to all PS Plus benefits and games on your PS5.

Can I play online with my own account?

+

Yes! Once the shared account is set as primary on your PS5, all accounts on that console can play online and access the PS Plus game library.

What happens when the subscription expires?

+

When your rental period ends, you can renew to continue access. We'll send you a reminder before expiration.

Is this account sharing legal?

+

Yes, PlayStation's primary console feature is designed to allow game and subscription sharing within households.
